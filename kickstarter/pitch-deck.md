## 1. Hero

**Tagline:** Travel like a local. Host like a pro. Pay in your currency.

Global Tour is the mobile marketplace that matches travelers with vetted local hosts for personalized city tours — with a multi-currency payments layer built from day one. Tourists pay in the currency they know, hosts get paid in theirs, and the platform settles transparently. No more FX guesswork, no more generic tours.

---

## 2. The Problem

Travel today is broken in three ways:

- **Tourists get generic experiences.** Big-group bus tours and templated itineraries miss the soul of a city. Travelers want authentic, human-scale experiences but lack a trusted way to find local hosts globally.
- **Local hosts have no fair global platform.** Independent guides juggle WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, and informal cash payments. They lose a slice of every booking to platforms that don't understand their local market.
- **Cross-border payments add friction and fees.** A tourist from Germany paying a host in Argentina faces opaque FX rates, chargebacks, and delays. Hosts wait days or weeks for payouts and lose 3–7% to conversion spreads.

---

## 3. The Solution

Global Tour is a mobile-first marketplace that solves all three:

- **Curated local hosts** in every major city, with profiles, reviews, and specialties (food, history, nightlife, architecture, photography).
- **Native multi-currency UX** — every price is shown in the tourist's currency and the host's currency, transparently.
- **Escrow-backed bookings** — funds are held until the tour is completed, then released automatically. Disputes go through a structured resolution flow.
- **Flat 2% platform fee** — the lowest in the industry. Hosts keep 98% of what tourists pay.

---

## 4. How It Works

| Step | What happens |
|------|--------------|
| 1. Discover | Tourist browses hosts by city, language, and interest. Sees prices in their own currency. |
| 2. Book | Tourist books a time slot. Funds are authorized and moved into escrow. |
| 3. Experience | Tourist meets the host. Tour happens. Both parties confirm completion in-app. |
| 4. Pay | Escrow releases to the host automatically (or after dispute resolution). Platform takes a flat 2% fee. |

---

## 5. Why Now

Four forces make this the right moment:

- **Post-pandemic travel boom.** International tourist arrivals reached ~1.4 billion in 2024, recovering to pre-2019 levels. Travelers are spending more and seeking deeper experiences.
- **Remote work & digital nomads.** Millions of workers now stay weeks or months in foreign cities. They need local connections beyond Airbnb and TripAdvisor.
- **Stablecoin & payment rails maturity.** Treasury tooling (USDT, USDC) and cross-border rails (Stripe Connect, Bridge.xyz, Wise) have matured enough to support a true multi-currency marketplace.
- **Host empowerment trend.** Solo operators and micro-entrepreneurs want platforms that respect their margins — the 2% era is here.
